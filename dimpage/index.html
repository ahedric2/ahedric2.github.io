Bio

My name is Elijah Hedrick. I am 20 years old. I grew up in Columbia, Tennessee, where I graduated Spring Hill High School, Class of 2019. My interests include climate action, religion and philosophy, psychology and sociology, history, art, and music. My favorite movie is The Thing (1982) and my favorite book is Dune by Frank Herbert.
